Green Bee v0.1 — Lite Overview

Gameplay

  • Turn-based strategy simulation
  • Isometric buildable grid
  • Buy & sell objects on tiles
  • Budget and income per turn
  • Three visitor groups with suitability logic
  • Marketing spend influences global interest
  • Ticket sales simulated over time

Visual Feedback

  • Suitability vs. Interest chart
  • Budget & income timeline
  • Ticket sales projection
  • Hover-based explanations

Teacher & Admin

  • Global object & marketing configuration
  • World-specific rules and limits
  • Enable/disable objects and marketing per world
  • Student accounts with access codes
  • Live teacher overview of all players

Technical

  • Persistent Firebase storage
  • Per-player game state
  • D3.js dynamic SVG rendering
  • Responsive layout
  • Light / Dark mode
